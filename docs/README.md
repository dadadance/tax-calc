## Important Notes

### Verification Required
⚠️ **Property Tax Threshold Discrepancy:**
- **Official RS.ge threshold:** 40,000 GEL (individual income)
- **Current calculator threshold:** 65,000 GEL
- **Action:** Verify with RS.ge whether:
  - Threshold should be 40,000 GEL (individual income)
  - Or 65,000 GEL applies to family income
  - Calculator may need update

### Tax Rate Verification
- **Property Tax Rate:** Up to 1% (typically 1%)
- **Current implementation:** 1% (0.01)
- **Status:** ✅ Confirmed correct
